Dosage: Take Metformin 500mg twice a day after meals (9 AM and 9 PM).

Uses: Helps control blood sugar levels and manage Type 2 diabetes.

Diet Plan: Avoid sugar and refined carbs (white bread, sweets). Eat fiber-rich foods (vegetables, whole grains, legumes).

Side Effects: Nausea, vomiting, stomach upset, metallic taste.

Reminder: Set alarms for 9 AM and 9 PM for medicine. Regularly check blood sugar levels.
