✅Cancer

Dosage: Take Tamoxifen 20mg once daily after breakfast, and follow up with Capecitabine 500mg twice a day as prescribed by your oncologist.

Uses: Helps shrink tumors, slow the spread of cancer cells, and improve quality of life during treatment.

Diet Plan: Eat a high-protein, antioxidant-rich diet (fruits, leafy greens, fish). Avoid processed foods, red meat, and alcohol. Stay hydrated.

Side Effects: Fatigue, hair loss, nausea, lowered immunity, mouth sores.

Reminder: Take Tamoxifen at 8 AM, Capecitabine at 8 AM and 8 PM. Don’t skip doses and strictly follow your chemo/radiation appointments.
